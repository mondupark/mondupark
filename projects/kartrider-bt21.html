About the Project

KartRider x BT21 collaboration is a commercial project combining Nexon's racing game and LINE Friends' BT21 characters. As a lookdev artist, I was responsible for texturing and shading BT21 characters and themed vehicles. I worked according to brand guidelines using Substance Painter, Maya, and V-Ray, collaborating with Nexon concept artists.
