Project Overview

'Guardians of the Video Game' is an animation series themed around gaming culture, implementing visuals transitioning between pixel world and realistic world. As a lookdev artist, I was responsible for two visual style work. I performed shader work for pixel art effects in Unreal Engine and texturing of main characters, participating in asset creation suitable for both pixel and realistic environments.
