About the Project

Guardians of the Video Game is an animated series that combines gaming culture with superhero action. As a character artist on this production, I was responsible for bringing the show's diverse cast of characters to life through detailed 3D modeling and texturing work.

The project required translating concept art into production-ready 3D assets that could be efficiently animated while maintaining visual appeal. Each character needed clean topology for deformation, well-organized UV layouts for texture painting, and materials that would render consistently across different lighting scenarios. Working closely with the rigging and animation teams, I ensured that all models met technical requirements for the production pipeline.

This series featured a mix of stylized human characters and fantastical elements, which allowed for creative problem-solving in both modeling and texturing workflows. The production was completed in 2024, with the series awaiting its official release date.
