About the Project

'Toemarok' is an animated film based on the original webtoon, pursuing painterly stylized visuals. As a lookdev artist, I was responsible for texturing and shading props. I participated in painterly style work using custom brushes in Substance Painter and stylized shader creation through Maya and V-Ray, supporting additional texture work in the latter part of the project.
