Project Overview

'Denma' is an SF animation series based on a popular webtoon, implementing the complex worldview and diverse characters in 3D. As a lookdev artist, I was responsible for creating various variations of characters and props. I efficiently produced dozens of variations required by the project while creating textures in Substance Painter and performing shading work in Unreal Engine.
