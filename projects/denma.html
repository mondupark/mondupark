About the Project

Denma is an upcoming sci-fi animation series based on the popular Korean webtoon. As a lookdev artist on this production, I'm contributing to the development of unique character visual styles that bring the distinctive comic aesthetic to life in 3D animation.

This project presents exciting challenges in translating 2D comic art into compelling 3D characters while maintaining the original visual identity. My focus is on developing sophisticated shaders and materials that capture the essence of the source material while taking advantage of modern rendering techniques. Each character requires careful attention to detail in both modeling and lookdev to ensure they feel authentic to the webtoon while working within the animation pipeline.

Working on an active production allows for continuous refinement and collaboration with the art direction team. The project is currently in production, with the series expected to release in 2025. The technical demands of sci-fi elements combined with character-focused storytelling create unique opportunities for innovative lookdev solutions.
