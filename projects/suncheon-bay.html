Project Overview

'Suncheon Bay Secret Adventure' is a 4D attraction film created for Suncheon Bay National Garden. As a lookdev artist, I was responsible for texturing work and Unreal Engine optimization of assets. I integrated outsourced and purchased assets to fit the 4D theater system and performed performance optimization work in a real-time rendering environment.
