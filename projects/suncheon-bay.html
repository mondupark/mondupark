About the Project

Suncheon Bay Secret Adventure is a 4D attraction film created for the Suncheon Bay National Garden, one of Korea's premier ecological tourism destinations. This project combined environmental storytelling with immersive 4D effects to create an engaging experience for visitors of all ages.

As an environment and character artist on this production, I was responsible for creating a diverse range of assets that brought the natural beauty of Suncheon Bay to life in 3D. This included detailed vegetation models, stylized character designs representing local wildlife, and atmospheric environmental elements. The challenge was to balance visual quality with the technical constraints of 4D theater playback systems.

Working on a 4D attraction required special considerations for camera movement, timing, and visual clarity, as the content needed to synchronize with physical effects in the theater. Our team developed a comprehensive asset library that could be efficiently rendered while maintaining the high visual standards expected for a major tourist attraction. The film premiered in 2023 and continues to delight visitors to Suncheon Bay National Garden.
