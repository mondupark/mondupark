Project Overview

'Yumi's Cells' Season 2 is a tvN drama combining live-action and 3D animation, Korea's first TV animation project utilizing Unreal Engine. As a lookdev team member, I was responsible for texturing and shading cell characters. I participated in implementing the series' stylized look using Substance Painter and Unreal Engine, and contributed to maintaining quality in a real-time rendering environment through collaboration with the modeling and lighting teams.
