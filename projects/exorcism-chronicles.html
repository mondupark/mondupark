About the Project

Toemarok is a supernatural horror film that required extensive VFX work to bring otherworldly creatures and paranormal phenomena to life. As part of the LOCUS VFX team, I specialized in creating realistic creature models and supernatural assets that seamlessly integrated with live-action footage.

The project demanded a unique blend of anatomical accuracy and fantastical design. Working closely with the VFX supervisor and concept artists, I developed a range of demon and spirit creatures that were both terrifying and believable. Each creature required detailed sculpting in ZBrush, followed by retopology and extensive texturing work to achieve the desired photorealistic quality.

One of the most challenging aspects was ensuring that our CG elements would hold up under various lighting conditions in the final composite. This required developing flexible shader systems and working iteratively with the lighting and compositing departments. The film was successfully released in 2024, with the VFX sequences receiving positive recognition for their realism and visual impact.
