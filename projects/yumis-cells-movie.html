Feature Film

Released

About the Project

'Yumi's Cells: The Movie' expanded the TV series for theatrical release, with the live-action world produced in Maya and V-Ray, and the cell village in Unreal Engine. As a lookdev artist, I was responsible for texturing and shading main characters including Bobby, Yumi, and Daeun. I implemented visuals for each world according to the characteristics of two rendering engines and worked on V-Ray shaders in Maya. I participated in creating theatrical-quality looks through collaboration with modeling and lighting teams.
