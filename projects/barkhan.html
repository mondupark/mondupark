About the Project

'BARKHAN' is an animation currently in production, implementing comic-style using Unreal Engine. As a lookdev artist, I am participating in comic-style shader work. I perform texturing and shading using Substance Painter and Unreal Engine, creating outline and hatching effects. I am implementing comic expression in real-time 3D through hatching lines using masks and custom outliner.
