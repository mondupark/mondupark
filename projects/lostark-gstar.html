About the Project

Lost Ark G-Star exhibition content was a promotional project commissioned by Smilegate RPG. As a lookdev artist, I was responsible for quality improvement of outsourced assets. I refined textures and materials of characters, weapons, and props to match Lost Ark's art direction using Maya and Substance Painter.
