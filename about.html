About Me

3D artist specializing in modeling, texturing, and lookdev for animation and games.

I create 3D assets from modeling to final lookdev, with over 3 years of production experience in TV animation, feature films, and commercial projects. My lookdev background ensures strong texturing and shading quality, combined with modeling capabilities for complete asset workflows.

I work with both stylized and realistic art styles, focusing on efficient workflows for real-time rendering. My technical toolkit includes Blender, ZBrush, Substance Painter, and Unreal Engine.
